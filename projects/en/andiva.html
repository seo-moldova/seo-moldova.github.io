The chart shows the website's transition from "moderate activity" to "intensive traffic" phase. The transformation process is divided into three key stages:

01

Moderate Activity State

In the first half of the period, the click line fluctuated in the lower third of the scale. The project had a stable but low traffic level, never exceeding a narrow corridor.

02

Structural Optimization Stage

After implementing changes, sustained growth began. Average traffic increased more than 2.5 times (+150%). The project stabilized above the middle of the scale.

03

Intensive Traffic Phase

Peak performance: maximum daily click values increased almost 4 times (+300%) compared to initial peaks. The project began regularly reaching the top of the chart.

Key Achievement: "floor line" elevation — current minimum indicators (dips) are now at or above the average values that existed before the active optimization phase. The result has stabilized and does not roll back.
