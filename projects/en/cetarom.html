The chart clearly illustrates the transformation of the resource from "invisible" to an active search asset. The process is divided into three qualitative development stages:

01

Zero Stage (Stagnation)

Long period of no visibility. Impressions and clicks were at minimal values, close to statistical noise. The project had no search authority.

02

Explosive Growth Stage (Breakthrough)

Sharp vertical jump in both curves. Impressions increased 10x+ — a direct result of mass page entry into the index across a wide range of keywords.

03

New Plateau Formation Stage

Transition to volatile but consistently high traffic levels. New graph lows are now significantly higher than previous period peaks.

Key Achievement: Traffic floor increased 6x, overall search visibility increased 10x. A solid foundation has been formed that generates a steady stream of organic leads.
