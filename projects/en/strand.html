Visual analysis of the curves (Clicks and Impressions) reveals three qualitative stages of the project:

01

Breaking the Glass Ceiling (Stagnation)

Long horizontal trend with fluctuations in a narrow corridor. The project couldn't break through the traffic resistance level — a classic situation for "old" sites with technical debt.

✅ Result: barriers identified and removed, base prepared for explosive growth.

02

Synchronous Scaling (Breakthrough Phase)

Sharp, almost vertical angle of both curves simultaneously. Impression growth immediately pulls clicks — the most important indicator of SEO quality.

✅ Result: optimization targeted high-intent, high-volume queries that the target audience actually searches for.

03

New Floor Formation (Stability)

Current lows at the end of the period are higher than the highest peaks at the beginning. Even during natural downturns, the project receives significantly more traffic.

✅ Result: a stable system created that doesn't "roll back" after major fixes.

Final Efficiency: the project has transitioned to a qualitatively different weight category. The current trend is accelerating growth, indicating accumulated site authority in search engines' eyes.
