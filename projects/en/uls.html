Panda penalty recovery

This case is an excellent example of how systematic work with narrow but high-conversion semantics (B2B/Digital services) delivers stable results, even after serious Google penalties.

📊

Google Analytics — Traffic Dynamics (2020-2023)

The site was under Google Panda penalties due to a large number of spammy backlinks. After a comprehensive audit and issue resolution, the site was successfully removed from the filter.

Result: the site started with ~200 users per month (early 2020) and grew to 1,200–1,500 users by the end of 2022. Net growth was +650%.

Trend characteristic: until mid-2021, the site was in "sleep" mode due to the penalties, after which stable linear growth began. This is the result of Google accumulating enough data about page relevance for digital marketing services after the filter was lifted.
