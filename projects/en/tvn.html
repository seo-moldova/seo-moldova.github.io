What Was Done

A comprehensive audit of the site's technical condition and semantic structure was conducted. Critical indexing issues were identified and resolved, and daily SEO support processes were established.

Key work areas:

📝

Content Optimization

Editing and optimizing new and existing materials, improving readability and semantic structure of articles

🔗

Technical SEO

Monitoring internal and external links, fixing broken URLs, maintaining sitemap and robots.txt

📊

Monitoring & Analytics

Tracking indexing, checking SEO tags, Google Search Console monitoring, daily SEO hygiene maintenance

📋

Detailed Work List

Content & Structure

  • Editing and optimizing new and existing materials
  • Improving readability and semantic structure of articles
  • Optimizing meta-data and titles for new content
  • Adding alt-text to images when necessary
  • Updating articles and media according to SEO guidelines
  • Creating templates for new content types

Technical SEO

  • Monitoring internal and external links, fixing broken URLs
  • Maintaining Sitemap and Robots.txt
  • Tracking indexing of new pages and media content
  • Optimizing internal linking structure
  • Fixing crawling errors in Google Search Console
  • Setting canonical tags for duplicate content

Monitoring & Analytics

  • Google Search Console monitoring for indexing errors and links
  • Checking SEO tags and titles for new publications
  • Daily technical SEO hygiene maintenance
  • Weekly reporting on key metrics
  • User behavior analysis in Google Analytics
  • Ranking monitoring for key queries
