The website was launched from scratch in July 2023. The chart demonstrates the classic journey from sandbox to stable traffic — an excellent example of successful SEO launch.

01

Index Entry (July — August 2023)

First clicks, growth from 0 to first stable values. No sandbox — the site started growing almost immediately after launch.

✅ Result: proper technical foundation — clean code, fast indexing, no errors in robots.txt / sitemap.

02

Organic Warm-up (September — December 2023)

Growth by +300% from start. Google began to trust the content, the site gained authority.

✅ Result: quality content and proper semantics ensured sustainable growth.

03

Explosive Growth (January 2024)

Jump by +100–150% in a single month. Likely the effect of accumulated link weight and top positions for high-volume queries.

✅ Result: total impressions growth by 1200%, clicks by 900% from start to peak.

04

Consolidation (February — April 2024)

Plateau maintenance, growth slowed, transitioning to quality phase — optimizing what already ranks in top positions.

✅ Result: stable top presence, foundation for further scaling.

Key Conclusion: Line synchronization — blue line (clicks) follows purple line (impressions) amplitude — confirms that targeted traffic is being attracted. No "empty" impressions for irrelevant queries.
