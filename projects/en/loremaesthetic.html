Based on the visual geometry of the chart from September 2023 to April 2024, three key transformation stages can be identified:

01

Stabilization (September — November)

Flat period with fluctuations of 0–10%. During this stage, audit and base preparation for future growth were conducted. The purple line was in the lower quarter of the chart.

02

Breakthrough (December — January)

Sharp visibility jump with growth rate +40–60% month over month. Classic sign of a successful SEO strategy: new page indexing and domain authority growth.

03

Scaling (February — April)

Consolidation at new positions with stable growth +15–20% month over month. By April, the purple line is consistently in the upper third of the chart — threefold growth in search visibility coverage.

Key Conclusions: The growth is not a "one-time spike" — we observe a baseline elevation. Even during downturns (weekends/holidays), new lows are higher than the peaks at the beginning. The blue click line grew from +200% to +250%, confirming traffic quality.
